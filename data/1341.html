Hooray -- I got a demotion! (Tuesday, 17 February 2004)  

Like any other business, SourceGear evolves.  Today I would like to let the world know about a change that has happened recently here at SourceGear.  This change is important but not highly visible to our customers.

As of January 1st, SourceGear has been restructured at the corporate level.  Previously, we were "SourceGear Corporation", but now we are "SourceGear LLC".  However, the important thing here is not the slight variation in the name, but rather, the major change in ownership.

Back in 1997, the first two people I hired were Dan Schreiber and Corey Steffen.  Since then, these two guys have become the backbone of our company.  Corey and Dan are the ones who make things happen here at SourceGear.  Furthermore, they participate in all major decisions of the company.  In the last two years, they have come to function less as my employees and more as my partners.  Last fall, I decided it was time that this arrangement be formalized.

It took some time to get all the legal work done, but the deal was completed on January 1st.  Instead of being the principal owner of SourceGear, I am now just one of three, and I could not be happier with the change.

Like I said, this change will be mostly invisible to the rest of the world.  Corey and Dan are a bit quieter than I am. 

Still, this is something I want people to know.
