CornSharp source code?
13 May 2003

Heh.  Phillip Pearson gives CornSharp a try, and wants to know if I will release source code.  I've had several other requests for the source as well.  In principle, I have no real objection to giving the source away.  I have no intention of trying to make money with this app.  However, there may be practical obstacles.

The primary problem is that CornSharp uses one commercial third party component (the SSH feature), and I'm thinking about using another one (a better WYSIWYG editor).  I might be able to separate those pieces into plugins and release the remainder.  This is only likely to happen if I get around to it.  I have a day job, so CornSharp gets my attention in randomly scheduled bursts. 

I do plan to continue improving CornSharp, because I use it every day, and it's pretty darn lame.
