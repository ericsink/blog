Case study on SourceGear and C# (Friday, 24 October 2003)  

Just in time for PDC 2003, Microsoft has posted a case study about SourceGear and our use of C# and the .NET Framework.  The document focuses primarily on our decision to port the SourceOffSite server from Java to C#, but also gives a bit of the background on how we came to be .NET fans by attending the PDC before this one.

A big thank-you from us to David Weller for helping make this case study happen.
