Oops (Monday, 20 October 2003)  

Earlier today I was fiddling with my blog software and the way I post things to my web server.  In the process, lots of items in my RSS feed now appear to be new, even though they are not.  Since I switched my aggregator to NewsGator, I've realized how annoying it is when a site does this. 

My apologies.  I'll try not to make a habit of this.
