07 Jan 2002

Thoughts on Apple's announcement today: The new iMac looks cool, but I'm not sure it lives up to the pre-show hype. They set our expectations higher, leading us to believe they were to announce something a lot more world-changing than a new computer. I agree with others who think the product should have been called iHype.

Still, they pulled off a very, very nice PR trick. How many new computers get the cover of Time magazine?

BTW, I think I'll end up buying one. We're building a new house, and I think the new iMac design will look great on the desk in our kitchen. :-)
