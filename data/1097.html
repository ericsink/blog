Actually it will take a few months for the space to be remodeled, so we're not moving right away. 

But it's official now.  We signed the lease today for new office space.  Sometime this fall, SourceGear will be relocating to the fourth floor of a classy old building in downtown Champaign, just down the street from Volition and just one mile from the world's smartest building.

We're really excited.  The new space is currently an empty shell but it's going to be set up just the way we want it, including a server room with extra cooling and special room for our pool table.  Every employee will have a private office with a window looking out over the downtown area.  Within a two block radius, we'll have a great selection of restaurants and coffee shops as well as a park.

I dread the actual move, but I'm so eager to start settling into our new location.
