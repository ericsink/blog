NotALegend.com is a parody of an advertising campaign Microsoft did during early 2003.  The original site was at www.software.legends.com, but now it's gone.

The WaybackMachine at archive.org has copies of the Software Legends site, but unfortunately a lot of the images are missing.

I'd love to get a complete copy of the original site.  If you have one, please let me know.  My parody just isn't quite as funny now that you can't see the object of the joke.  :-)
