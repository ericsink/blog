--- layout: post esbma_id: 1623 title: SourceGear Job Opening: Marketing Communications date: 2006-08-17 16:14:38 ---

As part of SourceGear's continued growth, we would like to hire someone to join our team in a marketing role.  The person we hire will end up being responsible for some combination of the following responsibilities:

  • Website management, including design, structure and content
  • Coordinate SourceGear participation in trade shows and/or conferences
  • Coordinate advertising, including print and web
  • Write press releases and other informational materials
  • Write product information materials for prospective customers
  • Design graphics for promotional materials
  • Manage production of printed materials
  • Manage brand creation and identity

The person we hire will have some combination of the following skills and experience:

  • Web design, HTML, CSS, Dreamweaver
  • Graphic design
  • Photoshop and Illustrator (or equivalents)
  • Production of printed materials
  • Trade show displays
  • Excellent written and spoken English
  • Bachelor's degree in a related field

In other words, we want to hire someone in Marketing Communications.  We're looking for someone who can take on a big challenge and finish, someone who knows how to make everything look really professional, someone who actually believes there are more than seven colors.  :-)

This is an ideal time to get involved with SourceGear.  You will work on the continuing marketing effort for Vault, but you will also be involved with the launch of a new product we haven't announced yet.

This position is located in Champaign, Illinois.

To apply for this job or ask questions about it, send your resume to jobs@sourcegear.com
