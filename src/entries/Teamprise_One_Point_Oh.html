Congratulations to the folks at Teamprise, a sister company to SourceGear.  They released version 1.0 of their product today.  Teamprise Client Suite includes three cross-platform clients for Microsoft Team Foundation Server:  an Explorer-style app, a command-line client, and an Eclipse plugin.
