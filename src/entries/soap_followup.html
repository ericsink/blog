I got several responses to my query about SOAP interop with ASP.NET.

  • Apache Axis was mentioned several times.  Some had good results with it, but not everyone.  Two people suggested that Axis does just fine as long as things don't get terribly complicated.
  • One person reported good results using SOAP::Lite from Perl.
  • One person mentioned gSoap with positive remarks.
  • One person reported that they were able to achieve good interop by simply hand-coding their own implementation.

Thanks!
