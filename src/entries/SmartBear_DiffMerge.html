--- layout: post esbma_id: 1698 title: DiffMerge to be Bundled with Smart Bear Products date: 2007-07-25 16:35:56 keywords: (sourcegear) ---

I am pleased to announce that SourceGear and Smart Bear Software have reached an agreement which will lead to the bundling of SourceGear DiffMerge with Smart Bear's products.

I have enjoyed getting to know Smart Bear's founder, Jason Cohen.  Some companies are just likeable.  The world often seems like a vast sea of companies which are devoid of personality.  And then every so often you run across a company like Smart Bear, where the company tag line at the bottom of their website is "All your code review are belong to us."  :-)

Anyway, Jason has a very cool company with several products for developers.  Starting in September, DiffMerge will be bundled with two of those products, Code Pickle and Code Collaborator.
