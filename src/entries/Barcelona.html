Greetings from Barcelona!  SourceGear is here as an exhibitor at TechEd this week.  I'm struggling with jet lag right now, but the coffee is strong and I can see the Mediterranean Sea out my hotel window.  Life is pretty good.

In nearly ten years of business, this is our first trade show outside the US.  We are very accustomed to apologizing for the fact that our product is English-only, but this week we can offer that apology in person.

Anyway, we chose this event to start talking about a new product, SourceGear Fortress.  In a nutshell, Fortress is an ALM solution designed specifically for small and medium-sized teams.  I posted an overview whitepaper in a separate entry.  The product is tentatively scheduled for release in March, but we're giving preview demos of Fortress in our booth this week.

In fact, I am typing this blog entry from our booth, which also happens to have a view of the Mediterranean Sea.  At this point, I'm thinking we need to come to this event again in the future.  :-)
