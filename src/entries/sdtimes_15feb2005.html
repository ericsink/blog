Today's issue of SD Times contains an article about our Allerton project.

As an ISV, working with the press is always interesting.  Their job is not to tell the story you want them to tell, but rather, to tell whatever story is most interesting for their readers.  Your inputs are a press release and a phone interview.  In the end, the output is an article which may or may not be what you envisioned.  If it's not, you console yourself by saying "there is no such thing as bad press".  :-)

This piece is actually pretty good, but it's not really an article about SourceGear.  It's an article about Microsoft and whether or not they are a good partner.  I can see how readers probably find that angle interesting.

Anyway, no real complaints here.  To Jennifer deJong and SD Times, thanks for the article!
