The Madrid bombings
11 Mar 2004

Sorry for the off-topic post, but the bombings in Madrid today are somewhat of a personal issue for me.

I used to live in the Madrid area.  In fact, I rode through all three of those train stations every day on my way to work.  I still have many friends there, and I hope to hear soon that they are all safe.  My condolences and prayers are with the victims and their families.
