The End of the Browser War?
30 May 2003

The AOL/Microsoft deal is being widely described as the end of the browser war.  I disagree.  Either the browser war is not over, or it was already over.

  • Because Mozilla is open source, the lizard can never be completely and 100% dead.  AOL/Netscape may abandon all support, but the codebase lives on.
  • You can argue that this deal makes the Netscape browser effectively dead, but that was true a long time ago.

Move along folks, move along, nothing to see here.
