Mono 0.28
02 Oct 2003

Congratulations to Miguel and all the Mono developers on the release of Mono 0.28.  This release contains all the work Ximian was doing as part of our agreement with them.  Working with Miguel and his team was a really positive experience, just as I expected it would be.  The project had two milestone deadlines and they hit them both well ahead of the scheduled dates.

At this point, Ximian has completed 90% of the work necessary to make the Vault command-line client work on Linux and its ilk.  Now we at SourceGear need to do the remaining 90%.
