--- layout: other esbma_id: 1348 date: 2004-07-23 14:44:38 keywords: (cornsharp) (daypage) ---  SourceGear wins Readers Choice Award from Visual Studio Magazine (Monday, 12 April 2004)  

With gratitude and a sheepish grin, I take this opportunity to mention that SourceGear won a Readers Choice Award at the recent VSLive show.

I wear the sheepish grin for several reasons. 

  • First of all, I had no idea we were nominated. 
  • Second, I have no idea who the competition was. 
  • Third, we won in a category which seems to be an odd fit for our products.

But the gratitude remains.  These awards are good-old-fashioned popularity contests, and we won.  It is always nice to be liked.
