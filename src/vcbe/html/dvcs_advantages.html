Chapter 5. Advantages

With obvious exceptions like furniture and Star Wars movies, new things tend to be better than old things. The state of the art advances. An industry’s more recent offerings usually incorporate better technology and practices.

The reason DVCS tools are the third generation of version control is that they are in several ways better than the second generation offerings. In this chapter I will discuss some of the advantages a DVCS can provide over the centralized tools.
